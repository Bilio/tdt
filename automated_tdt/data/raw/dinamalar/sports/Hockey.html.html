Do you want to make SPORTS your homepage?

In Firefox :
  • In the TOOLS menu, select OPTIONS.
  • At the top of the dialog box, select the GENERAL tab.
  • In the HOME PAGE text box, type http://sports.dinamalar.com/, then click OK.

Do you want to make SPORTS your homepage?

In Chrome :
  • 1. Select the Chrome wrench icon at the top of your browser window. From the drop-down menu that appears, select OPTIONS.
  • 2. At the top of the dialog box, select the BASICS tab.
  • 3. In the HOME PAGE section , type http://sports.dinamalar.com, in the OPEN THIS PAGE text box, then click CLOSE.
Close
